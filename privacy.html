Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01.10.2024

Thank you for using Messagram. This Privacy Policy explains how Code Hospital ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. SMS Data

Our App collects SMS messages sent to your phone in order to forward them to your Telegram account. This data includes:

  • The content of the SMS messages.
  • The sender’s phone number.

b. Device Information

We may collect certain device-specific information, such as:

  • Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • IP address and device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • SMS Forwarding: We collect SMS messages to forward them to your designated Telegram account.
  • Support and Maintenance: Device information is used to improve our App’s functionality and user experience.

We do not share, sell, or distribute your SMS data with third parties for any purposes except as required by law.

3. Storage and Security of Data

We take the security of your data seriously. Your SMS messages are processed locally on your device and are only transmitted to your designated Telegram account. We do not store any SMS data on our servers.

We use commercially reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Your Consent

By using our App, you consent to the collection and use of your information as outlined in this Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

We only retain your data for as long as necessary to provide the service of forwarding SMS messages to Telegram. Once the message is forwarded, no SMS data is stored by us.

6. Third-Party Services

Our App may interact with third-party services such as Telegram. Your interactions with those services are governed by their own privacy policies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information that we hold.
  • Withdraw consent at any time for the collection and processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@messagram.org.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App. Your continued use of the App after any modifications to this policy will constitute your acknowledgment of the modifications and your consent to abide by the updated policy.
